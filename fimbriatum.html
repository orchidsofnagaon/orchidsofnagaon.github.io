Dendrobium fimbriatum

Fig: Dendrobium fimbriatum

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Dendrobinae
  • Genus:          Dendrobium
  • Species:        fimbriatum

CHARACTERSTICS

Hardy can type orchid.Stem erect, cylindrical, stout, gradually tapering towards the apex, about 1m high Leaves lanceolate, sessile, deciduous, 8-12 x 1-1.5cm. Inflorescence racemes, 1-3 pendulous, 15-20cm long inflorescence having 8-12 flowers in each. Flowers are about 7.5cm across, bright yellow, mildly scented; sepal; subcoriaceous, yellow; dorsal sepal elliptic, apex rounded, 5 nerved, 25 x 17mm; lateral sepals acute, keeled at the apex, 28 x 18mm; lip orbicular, fimbriate, large dark reddish-brown blotch at the base, 28 x 26mm; pollinia 2mm long.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Attactive Orchid, suitable for cut flower as well as garden.

⦁ Flowering time : April-May.
