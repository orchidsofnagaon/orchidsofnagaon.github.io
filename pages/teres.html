Papilionanthe teres (Roxb.) Schltr.

Fig: Papilionanthe teres (Roxb.) Schltr.

(Image Source: efloraofindia)

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Sarcanthinae
  • Genus:         Papilionanthe
  • Species:        teres

CHARACTERSTICS

A giant epiphytic orchid of about 5 meter height. Stems slender, terete, many noded, jointed. Leaves fleshy, teret, slender, 10-20 cm long. Infloresence racemes with 2-6 flowers. Flowers large, attractive, pinkish white, 10cm across, sepals subequal, light red to pinkish white, 10 cm across; sepals subeqaul, light red to pink; dorsal sepal ovate to elliptic, 4.5 x 3.5 cm; lateral sepals attatched to the foot of the column, 4.8 x 3.5cm; petals sub-orbicular, undulate; lip 3-lobed, lateral lobes bending over column, yellow with purple striations inside, milobed clawed and bilobed, rose purple, 6 x 7cm; column stout, erect; pollinia 2, ovoid.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable candidate for cut flower.

⦁ Flowering time : February-April.
