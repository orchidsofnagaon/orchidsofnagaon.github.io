Aerides odorata

Fig: Aerides odorata

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Sarcanthinae
  • Genus:          Aerides
  • Species:       odorata

CHARACTERSTICS

Stem profusely branched, pendulous, 40-60cm long. Leaves oblong-lanceolate, distichous, 20-25cm long. Infloroscence racemes, laxly many flowered. Flowers waxy white with tinge of purple, 2.5-3cm across; sepals reflexed, fleshy, up-curved apex, white; dorsal sepals 1.3 x 0.9cm; lateral sepals sub-falcate, 1.2- 1.5 x 0.9-1cm; petals white, rounded, oblong to ovate, 1-1.2 x 0.4-0.6cm; lip white with pinkish violet blotches, trilobed, 0.8-1 x 0.7-0.9cm; column white; pollinia yellow, globose.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Magnificient Orchid with sweeet fragnance, suitable for cut flower and perfume indusrty.

⦁ Flowering time : April-May.
