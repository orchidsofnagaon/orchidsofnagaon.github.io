Dendrobium nobile

Fig: Dendrobium nobile

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Dendrobinae
  • Genus:          Dendrobium
  • Species:        nobile

CHARACTERSTICS

An epihytic Orchid. Pseudobulbs are erect, tufted, about 25-40cm long. Leaves several, 8-12 x 2.5-3cm, ovate-lanceolate, obliquely notched at the apex. 14-20 flowers are arranged in a 25-30cm long spike. Flowers are 5-8cm across, sepals white margin with purple at the apex, 6mm x 2.7cm; petals 1cm x 2.7cm; lip transversely-oblong, pubescent, with deep violet patches and ribs are present on white background, 1cm x 2.7cm; pollinia 2, golden yellow in colour.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Bears high aesthetic value, suitable candidate for garden flower.

⦁ Flowering time : March-April.
