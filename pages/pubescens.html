Eria pubescens (Hook) Lindl.

Fig: Eria pubescens (Hook) Lindl.

(Image Source: efloraofindia)

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Epidendrinae
  • Genus:          Eria
  • Species:        pubescens

CHARACTERSTICS

Epiphytic orchid. Pseudo bulbs ovoid, furrowed with 4-5 leaves. Leaves narrowly oblong-lanceolate, acute, 15-20 x 1.5-3cm long. Infloroscene raceme, 3-8 flower in peduncle, greenish yellow, 2.5cm across; sepals larger than petals, hairy on the outer surface pale yellow-green, 1.5 x 0.6cm, petals obtuse, pale yellow, three nerved, 9 x 3mm; lip greenish yellow with purple spot, purple stripe along the mid nerve, trilobed, 12mm long and 8mm broad; anthers unglobose; pollinia 8, yellow.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ No asthetic value.

⦁ Flowering time : July-August.


GEOGRAPHICAL DISTRIBUTION

⦁ Amsoi

⦁ Chikoni Hills

⦁ Diju valley

⦁ Hatimura Hill

⦁ Kaziranga National Park

⦁ Laokhowa Wildlife Sanctuary
