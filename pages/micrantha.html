Smitinandia micrantha

Fig: Smitinandia micrantha

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Sarcanthinae
  • Genus:          Smitinandia
  • Species:        micrantha

CHARACTERSTICS

Epiphytic Orchid. Stem erect, stout, compressed 8-20cm long. Leaves oblong, sessile,keeled,bifid at the apex, 4-8, 5-12 x 1.2-1.8cm. Inflorescence raceme, 8-12cm long. Flowers pinkish white with large pink spots, 5mm across; sepals subequal, broadly ovate, 3.5mm long; petals smaller, oblong, spreading, purple, 3.5 x 1.8mm long; lip 3-lobed, fleshy, pinkish purple; column short stout, footless; anther depressed; pollinia 2, obovoid.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable candidate for cut flower.

⦁ Flowering time : July-August.
