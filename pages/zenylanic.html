Luisia zenylanic

Fig: Luisia zenylanic

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Sarcanthinae
  • Genus:          Luisia
  • Species:        zenylanic

CHARACTERSTICS

Epiphytic Orchid. Stem erect, tufted, 10 - 16cm long, dichotomously branched. Leaves terete, deep green, rigid, pointed at the apex, 10-15cm long. Inflorescence short, with 5-6 flowers per raceme. Flowers small, 0.5cm across; sepals and petals subequal; sepals 0.6-0.65 cm long; petals 0.65cm long; lip heart shaped, deep violet; pollinia 2, globose.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Flowers are small hence no aesthetic value.

⦁ Flowering time : June-July.
