Bulbophyllum caryanum (W.J.Hook) Spreng.

Fig: Bulbophyllum caryanum (W.J.Hook) Spreng.

(Image Source: efloraofindia)

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Cryptodiinae
  • Genus:         Bulbophyllum
  • Species:       caryanum

CHARACTERSTICS

Epiphytic Orchid with pseudobulb. Pseudobulb ovoid, bearing one leathery leaf. Leaves lathery, oblong-lanceolate, erect. Inflorescenes, Flowers blakish brown, 5mm across; sepals subeuals, acute; dorsal, sepals 4mm long, lateral sepals ovate 6x5mm long; petals ovate, acuminate, 1.5mm long; lip rilobbed, 2.5mm long; column winged; pollinia 4 in two pairs, orange yellow.
