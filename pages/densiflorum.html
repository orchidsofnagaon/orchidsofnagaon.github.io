Geodorum densiflorum (Lam.) Schltr.

Fig: Geodorum densiflorum (Lam.) Schltr.

(Image Source: efloraofindia)

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Cryptodiinae
  • Genus:          Geodorum
  • Species:        densiflorum

CHARACTERSTICS

Terrestrial Orchid. Pseudobulb tuberous, ovoid or obconical, green. Leaves 2-3, broadly elliptic to oblong -lanceolate, acute or subacuminate, 12-25 x 3.5-7cm, green. Inflorescence racemes, few flowered. Flowers white to slightly blue-reddish, 1.5-2cm across; sepals subequal, acute; dorsal sepal 11 x 3.2mm; lateral sepal 10 x 3mm; petals obtuse or subacute, 11-13 x 5-7mm; lip cymbiform, three distinct nerved with a yellow spot, 1.5cm long; column white, incurved, short, stout; anther two chambered; pollinia 2, orange-yellow, globose.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable plant for gardening.

⦁ Flowering time : July-August.


GEOGRAPHICAL DISTRIBUTION

⦁ Diju valley

⦁ Hatimura Hill

⦁ Kaziranga National Park
