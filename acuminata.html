Malaxis acuminata

Fig: Malaxis acuminata

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Liparidinae
  • Genus:          Malaxis
  • Species:        acuminata

CHARACTERSTICS

Terrestrial hearbs. Stems 10-12cm high. Leaves 3-4, thin, wavy in margin, 8-12cm long. Inflorescence racemes bearing large number of sparsly arranged flowers. Flowers yellowish green to purplish green, 10-12mm long; dorsal sepal narrow, subacute; lateral sepals broader, obtuse; petals linear; lip ovate, flattened; pollinia 8.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable candidate for gardening.

⦁ Flowering time : May-June.
