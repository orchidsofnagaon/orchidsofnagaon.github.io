Dendrobium aphyllum

Fig: Dendrobium aphyllum

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Dendrobinae
  • Genus:          Dendrobium
  • Species:        aphyllum

CHARACTERSTICS

Stem slender, pendulous, 52-138cm long, many noded, internodes 3.5 to 5cm long, striated, thick. Leaves sessile, 8 x 2.9cm, lanceolate to ovate, acute. Flowers light pink,upto 4.3cm across. Sepals oblong, lanceolate, subacute; dorsal sepals 3cm x 6.5-0.7cm, oblong acute, pale yellow at the apex with purple line at the base; lip shorty clawed at the base of the column ; Column short, white, operculum 2mm, pollinia 4, yellow in two closely colateral pairs.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable candidate for gardening.

⦁ Flowering time : February-March.
