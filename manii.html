Dendrobium mannii

Fig: Dendrobium mannii

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Dendrobinae
  • Genus:          Dendrobium
  • Species:        mannii

CHARACTERSTICS

Epiphytic Orchid. Stem compressed, many noded, 10-24cm long; internodes greenish yellow, leafy or leafless. Leaves densiform, acute, 2-3.5 x 0.6-1cm. Flowers 1-3, from the apex of the leafy stems or solitary at several nodes from the old leafless stem, 1.5 cm long; dorsal sepals ovate, lanceolate, 6 x 3cm; lateral sepals 4.5mm broad, petals narrower than the sepals, pale white, linear-oblong, 5 x 1mm; lip sessile at the base of the column, recurved towards apex; column short; pollinia 4 in pairs.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable candidate for garden flower.

⦁ Flowering time : December-January.
