Dendrobium formosum

Fig: Dendrobium formosum

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Dendrobinae
  • Genus:          Dendrobium
  • Species:        formosum

CHARACTERSTICS

Stem stout, many noded, erect, 20-45 x 12-15mm; sheaths covered with black hairs. Leaves oblong, unequally lobed at the apex, 7.4-14.4cm x 2.3-3.2cm. Inflorescence racemes, 3-4 flowers. Flowers large, white, 7-15cm across, sepals oblong-lanceolate, acute-accuminate, white, 5.5 x 2.0cm; petals broader, subarbicular, margins undulate, 5.5x4.5cm, white; lip broadly ovate, lateral lobes obscure, rounded; midlobe dialated, apiculated, 6.7cm long, column stout, white; anther quadrate.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Collector delightfull species.

⦁ Flowering time : May-June.
