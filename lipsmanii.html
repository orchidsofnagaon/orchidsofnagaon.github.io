Liparis mannii

Fig: Liparis mannii

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Liparidinae
  • Genus:          Liparis
  • Species:        manii

CHARACTERSTICS

Rare epiphyte. Pseudobulb ovoid, subcrompressed. Leaves terminal, sessile or subsessile, acute. Inflorescence solitary racemes, 12-13cm long. Flowers small, 0.5-0.7cm across, yellowish green; sepals subacute or obtuse, with recurved margins; petals linear, blunt; lip 3 lobed, subacute, sessile, shorter than the petals; column terete; anther acute.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable plant for gardening.

⦁ Flowering time : June-July.
