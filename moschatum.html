Dendrobium moschatum

Fig: Dendrobium moschatum

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Dendrobinae
  • Genus:          Dendrobium
  • Species:        moschatum

CHARACTERSTICS

A robust growing plant with tuft of pseudobulb attaining a length of about 1 to 2mm. Leaves oblong, acute. Inflorescence laxly, bears about 8-10 flowers. Flowers 8cm across, sweetle scented, orchraceous with purplish tinge on petals and sepals and reddish veins, sepals 3.5 x 1.5cm; petals 3.6 x 2.3 cm; lip shorter than the petals, slipped shaped, with two large brownish red spot inside, 3 x 1.8cm; column stout, yellow; pollinia 2.7mm long.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable candidate for cut flower.

⦁ Flowering time : May-June.
