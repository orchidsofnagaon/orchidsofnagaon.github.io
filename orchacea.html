Acampe orchacea

Fig: Acampe orchacea

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Sarcanthinae
  • Genus:          Acampe
  • Species:       orchracea

CHARACTERSTICS

⦁ Stems usually 30-80cm long. Leaves sessile, oblong, obtuse bilobed, 15-20 x 2-3cm. Infloroscence lateral in a panicle, erect, laxly many flowered. Flowers pale yellowish brown, 8-10mm across; sepals oblong to lanceolate, subequal 6-8 x 3-4mm; petals oblong-lanceolate, rounded at apex, lip trilobed, white whith a greenish yellow spout; column short, stout; pollinia 2, yellow, globose, 0.5-0.6mm.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A suitable candidate for garden flower.

⦁ Flowering time : December-January
