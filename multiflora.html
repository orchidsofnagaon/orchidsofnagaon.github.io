Aerides multiflora

Fig: Aerides multiflora

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Sarcanthinae
  • Genus:          Aerides
  • Species:       multiflora

CHARACTERSTICS

Stems usually curved, covered with stout leaf sheaths. Leaves oblong, leathery, 19 x 3.5cm. Inflorescence racemes, densly flowered, pendent; penduncle green. Flowers reddish, fragrant, 2.5cm across; sepals and petals subequal; dorsal sepal oblong, rounded and emarginate at the apex, 0.9 x 0.7cm; lateral sepals obutuse, obovate, 0.8 x 0.7cm petals elliptic oblong, emarginate, 1 x 0.6cm; lip trilobed, reddish-purple with dark red transverse treaks; pollinia 2, globular, yellow.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Attractive Orchid, suitable for cut flower.

⦁ Flowering time : April-May.
