Dendrobium lituiflorum

Fig: Dendrobium lituiflorum

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Dendrobinae
  • Genus:          Dendrobium
  • Species:        lituiflorum

CHARACTERSTICS

Stem pendulous, about 60cm long. Leaves linear-oblong, abscent during flowering. Flowers 2-3 or solitary from nodes of the stem, purple, upto 6cm across; sepals subcoriaceous, purple dorsal sepals brodly acute, 3.5 x 1cm; lateral sepals obliquely oblong, 3.8 x 1cm; lip white purple tranverse stripes, trumpet-shaped, 3.5 x 1.5cm; column white; pollinia 2mm long.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Magnificient Orchid, suitable for cut flower.

⦁ Flowering time : March-April.
