Acampe papillosa

Fig: Acampe papillosa

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Sarcanthinae
  • Genus:          Acampe
  • Species:       papillosa

CHARACTERSTICS

Epiphytic herb. Stem long, sheath below. Leaves sessile, 9-13 x 2-3.5cm, oblong, apex obliquely bilobbed. Inflorescence corymbose, penduncle short many flowered. Flowers white with yellow transverse bars dense, 6mm across, sepals and petals subequal 7mm long; lip sessile at the base of the column, erect acute; column short; pollinia 2, globose, 1.2mm long.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Bears attractive fragrance and hence is important in perfume industry.

⦁ Flowering time : December-January
