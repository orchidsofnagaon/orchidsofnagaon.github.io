Dendrobium jenkensii

Fig: Dendrobium jenkensii

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Dendrobinae
  • Genus:          Dendrobium
  • Species:        jenkensii

CHARACTERSTICS

Epiphytic Orchid Stem aggregated, flattened with solitary leaves. Leaves oblong-elliptic, 2.5 x 1cm. Infloroescence 1-2 flowered racemes. Flowers orange yellow, 3cm across; sepals subeqal, 5 nerved; dorsal sepals obtuse, 1.5 x 0.7cm; laterals sepals acute, 1.8 x 1cm; petals 1 nerved, reticulate, 1.6cm long, 1.2cm broad; lip subarbicular, 2.4 x 2.1cm, yellow, with an orange-yellow heart and pale yellow, heartand pale yellow margins; column stout; pollinia oblong, 1.55mm long, yellow.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ Flowering time : April-May.
