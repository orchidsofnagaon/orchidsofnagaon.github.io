Phaius tankervilleae

Fig: Phaius tankervilleae

  • Family:         Orchidaceae
  • Sub family:  Orchidoideae
  • Tribe:            Epidendreae
  • Sub tribe:     Liparidinae
  • Genus:          Phaius
  • Species:        tankervilleae

CHARACTERSTICS

A robust, ground Orchid. Pesudobulb conical, 10-15cm long, sheathed by leaf bases. Leaves large, 30-75 X 12-20cm, elliptic-lanceolate, strongly nerved. Inflorescence racemes, growing upto 150cm, 10-20 plowers each spike. Flowers large, 10-15cm across, buff coloured with a hue of brown, white towards the margin; sepals and petals red-brown inside white outside; lip trumpet shaped, white with orange yellow base streaked with red; column thin; pollinia 8.


IMPORTANCE

⦁ A good cut flower and garden flower.

⦁ Flowering time : April-May.
